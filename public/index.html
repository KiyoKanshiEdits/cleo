Why does exposure matter?
Having your on-chain financial behaviour public means you have no control over who sees your activity. This creates an unsecure trading environment leaving you vulnerable to threat actors and on-chain sleuths.
How can I protect myself?
Private and shielded trading is one way you can protect yourself from the ill-intentioned and ensure your success does not become your weakness.
How can I stay up to date on my exposure?
Cleo gives real-time information about the level of exposure for any of your wallets. Check regularly to see if any of your wallets become compromised by too much exposure.
